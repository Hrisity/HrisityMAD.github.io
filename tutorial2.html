Tutorial 2: Using Framer AI to Build a Simple Website Layout

If you’re a beginner who wants to learn how to create seamless layouts, or someone who wants to spend less time prototyping the layout, this tutorial is for you. In this tutorial, we will be using Framer AI to create a simple webpage. To achieve this, you will need to follow a few steps. However, before starting, you need to know what your website is for, and some ideas about how you envision the webpage.

Step 1: Start with Framer

First, open the Framer website, or the desktop app and create a new page. Then, click on the “search” option and select generate page:

Step 2: Insert Your Prompt

Secondly, insert the prompt for your desired webpage. In the example case, I am trying to build a landing page for a cosmetics online shop’s newest product. Here’s the prompt; you can modify the variables in the brackets, so it works for your project:

A [landing page] for an [online shop for cosmetic products presenting a new product - shampoo]. The name of the [shampoo is Rose Magic]. [Add a description and title on the right side of the shampoo bottle image, as well as an order now button]. The color palette should be [pink-lilac].

Here's a video showing how to do this:

As you can see, it generated a fully responsive webpage that looks seamless on different screen size devices. Based on the description, Framer AI generated the name of the product and placed it in the hero section, as well as text content that is related to the product. Of course, all that is customisable, and can be either fully changed or slightly modified. The photo placeholder are great as they allow us to replace them with photos of the real product (in this case). The button was also added and it works perfectly when linked to a corresponding page.

Overall, this is a tool with great potential that saves us a lot of time experimenting with layouts and allows us to focus on other features and details. Give it a try, the possibilities are endless. Enhance the ai-generated layout and create unique webpages that tell a story.
